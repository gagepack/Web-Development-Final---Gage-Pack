Why I Love Soccer

Soccer has been a huge part of my life. I love how simple the game is — just a ball, a field, and a group of players — but how strategic it becomes once you pay attention to spacing, movement, and decision making.

As a pre-business student, I also see soccer as a “team-based strategy problem.” Good teams communicate, adapt under pressure, and play to each players strengths. Those are the same skills that matter in business.

Top 3 Things About Soccer

  1. The Flow of the Game
    • Constant movement and quick decisions.
    • Switching the field to create space.
  2. Team Chemistry
    • Knowing where teammates will be without looking.
    • Trusting everyone to do their job.
  3. Big Moments
    • Last-minute goals.
    • Penalty shootouts and high-pressure situations.
